About me

I love to build. I find great joy in turning imaginative concepts into functional realities that solve problems. When it comes to web development, software and data engineering, I am a dedicated enthusiast. I love coming up with creative ideas and finding practical ways to bring them to life. It's like turning imagination into something you can actually click and use!"



My journey started in Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe, where I come from. It started with a curiosity to learn everything I can about technology and leverage it to solve problems. Over time, I have garnered a range of tech skills including Python, C, HTML, CSS, React and SQL. Currently, I am a sophomore pursuing a computer science degree at the University of Notre Dame



This portfolio showcases some projects where I have used these skills. At this stage of my career, I try as much as possible to work on projects that either I or someone could possibly remember one day and make positive use of.

Lets us connect! Feel free to contact me via any of my socials linked below. I look forward to collaborating with you!

Weather App

A web app that gives you the average temperature, wind spean, humidity and cloud cover for any town or city on that particular day. Simply enter your desired location and hit the search!

I used HTML and CSS to design the front-end, and JavaScript to develop the back-end. All data was sourced from Open Weather's public API and site is currently hosted by GitHub. Check it out!
